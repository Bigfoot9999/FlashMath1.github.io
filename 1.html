Super Smash Flash 2 is the second edition of the popular fighting game Super Smash Flash. A unique rendition of Nintendo's immensely successful Super Smash Bros, it features characters from Megaman to Naruto to Black Mage. The game plays as a fighting game, with 2 to 4 players on a map brawling to the death. Taking damage will increase a player's damage percent, ranging from 0% to 999%. As a player's damage percent increases they become more prone to knockback. Players will be killed whenever they fall off of the map or they are knocked too far in any direction. The game also has several modes such as stock(lives), time limit, and stadium.
